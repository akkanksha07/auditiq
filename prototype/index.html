Methodology

The Beneish M-Score

Developed by Professor Messod Beneish at Indiana University, the M-Score is a mathematical model that uses financial ratios to identify whether a company has manipulated its earnings. A score above −1.78 indicates a likely manipulator.

M = -4.84 + 0.920×DSRI + 0.528×GMI + 0.404×AQI + 0.892×SGI
      + 0.115×DEPI − 0.172×SGAI + 4.679×TATA − 0.327×LVGI
